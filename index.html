About

The Franklin is the West London Free School Sixth Formâ€™s student newspaper. Founded and run by pupils, it aims to provide a platform which enables them to explore relevant topics and express their own opinions - allowing them to both showcase their knowledge and learn from that of others. The newspaper covers an expansive range of topics; arts and humanities, politics, science and technology, book/film reviews, and sports, touching on wide variety of subjects, in order to ensure that there is something every student is passionate about. Published termly, each issue will offer a different perspective on current global issues, as well as more niche topics of interest, which we hope will be both informative and engaging. On behalf of the Franklin team, we hope you enjoy!
